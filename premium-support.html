Multi-timeframe context (4H + 1H) included.

AI Options Interpreter

Goes beyond simple timeframe alignment. Calculates weighted confluence across 5 timeframes (1D×4, 4H×3, 1H×2), HTF bias filter, market regime detection (trending/ranging/transition), and outputs CALL/PUT/AVOID with a specific expiry from real available strikes (15m, 30m, 1hr, 2hr, 4hr, Daily, Weekly). Confidence score 0–100.

1× Free Daily ✦ Unlimited Premium

AI Intermarket Analysis

Analyses correlations between asset classes — DXY vs Gold vs Oil vs Equities vs Bonds — to identify macro regime and cross-asset divergences. Tells you when currency strength or commodity positioning contradicts your setup, or confirms it. Essential context for Forex and commodity traders.

1× Free Daily ✦ Unlimited Premium

Reads the economic calendar and tells you which upcoming events are most likely to move your specific positions — not just generic impact ratings. Forecasts probable market reaction, suggests which pairs are most exposed, and recommends whether to hold, reduce, or avoid trading around each event.

06 — FAQ

Questions


What counts as "1 AI analysis per day" on the free tier? ▶
Each AI module counts separately. You get one request per day for Options Interpreter, Intermarket Analysis, and Calendar Intelligence. AI Elliott Wave Analysis is exclusive to Premium — free users have access to the live Elliott Wave setups feed instead. The daily counters reset at midnight UTC. Premium removes all limits.
Is the free tier really free forever? ▶
Yes. The free tier is permanent — no trial period, no credit card required. XenosFinance will always have a free tier. Premium is for traders who want to use the AI tools intensively throughout the trading day.
What is the AI Intermarket Analysis exactly? ▶
It analyses relationships between major asset classes — US Dollar Index (DXY), Gold, Crude Oil, S&P 500, US Treasuries, and key Forex pairs — to identify when macro flows confirm or contradict your trade setup. For example, it might flag that DXY strength is contradicting a EUR/USD long, or that Gold and Oil are both showing risk-off signals that support a defensive position.
How does the Weekly Trading Plan PDF work? ▶
Every Monday, a new Trading Plan PDF is published covering the week's key setups — Elliott Wave structure on major pairs, key levels, event risk from the calendar, and a directional bias for the week. Premium subscribers can download it directly from the platform.
Can I cancel anytime? ▶
Yes — cancel any time and your access continues until the end of the billing period. No lock-ins, no cancellation fees.
