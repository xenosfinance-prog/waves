Concurrently, rising Eurozone bond yields signal shifting investor sentiment away from safe havens toward yield-bearing assets.


GEOPOLITICAL RISK

Escalating maritime warfare in the Strait of Hormuz poses severe risks to global LNG and oil supply chains after successive attacks on commercial and Saudi-flagged tankers. In Eastern Europe, Russia increasingly frames its military campaign as a direct conflict with NATO amid ongoing frontline challenges. Meanwhile, domestic political tightening in China is highlighted by reports of an economist being detained and censored for delivering a pessimistic economic outlook.


MARKET OUTLOOK

Over the next 24 to 48 hours, traders must closely monitor the release of US CPI data and the Federal Reserve Minutes for definitive interest rate direction. The equity market faces a major technical event on Tuesday as SpaceX officially enters the Nasdaq-100, which is expected to expand the volatility gap against the S&P 500. Additionally, the Brent and WTI crude channels require continuous monitoring for any retaliatory military actions or shipping halts in the Strait of Hormuz.
